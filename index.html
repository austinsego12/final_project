Welcome

This home page gives visitors a simple way to get to know me and see the different parts of my life and interests.

The home page introduces these ideas and helps visitors understand who I am as both a student and a creator.

This website is designed to share my interests and goals. I enjoy working with computers, creating digital content, designing layouts, writing code, and exploring new ideas that help me improve. I want people to see my creativity, my personality, and the things that motivate me. My goal is to continue learning skills that will help me succeed in computer science and web development. This home page gives a clear view of what matters to me, what kind of work I enjoy, and where I want to go next.
